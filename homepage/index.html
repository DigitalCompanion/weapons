Applications

ImageSpace ImageSpace provides the ability to analyze and search through large numbers of images. These images may be text searched based on associated metadata and OCR text or a new image may be uploaded as a foundation for a search.

FacetView FacetView allows the investigation of large data sets based on the extraction and manipulation of relevant facets. These facets may be almost any consistent piece of information that can be extracted from the dataset: names, locations, prices, etc.

Banana - Ads Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive dashboards and custom searches. Included features: highlight terms, save searches and easily make your custom dashboards.

Banana - Images Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive dashboards and custom searches. Included features: highlight terms, save searches and easily make your custom dashboards.

WeaponsViz Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive D3 charts and custom searches. Included features: make your own query, custom date range and filter top results. Exploratory analysis on collected advertisements and images from the Weapons domain.

Geoparser is an open source tool that can process information from any file or solr index, extract geographic coordinates, and visualize locations on a map.
